Ez Styles


Ez Styles is a quick-check service to see if what you're wearing is A-OK. The specific principles are available for those curious!

Style Guide

Style Rules


This application follows a few simple logical rules to decide on the validity of the user's getup. Though simple individually, the rules must be followed in conjunction and adapt to the components selected


Color definitions:


Monochromatic:

 

Chromatic:

 


  1. 1. No onesies! Wearing all of one color is not matching.
  2. 2. Ensemble pieces must match (eg. Hat, Belt, Shoes, Watch)
  3. 3. An outfit can only have 2 chromatic pieces max
  4. 4. Seperate pieces of the same color are counted as additional chromatic pieces

Note: This service does not as of yet take into account patterns or form-fit.
